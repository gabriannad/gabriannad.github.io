Yelp Contributor Onboarding

June–August 2017
  • Sketch
  • Principle

In order to help new Yelp users discover and understand the value of some of Yelp’s core actions, my team built an experience on the Me tab of the mobile app that requires users to do some lightweight tasks in order to achieve profile completion. As the sole designer on the Contributions team at the time, I designed the entire experience from start to finish.
