Spotter

September–December 2014
  • Paper prototyping
  • Illustrator

As a student of the user interface class I took at UC Berkeley in the fall of 2014, I worked on a team of five to ideate, research, design, and prototype a phone and smartwatch application for our final class project. Our application, Spotter, is a weight lifter’s end-to-end workout companion. It utilizes the mobile and smart watch form factors for a seamless experience through all three stages of a weight lifter’s workout. Users can create and manage their routines before hitting the gym, receive step-by-step guidance through a workout, and get detailed personal analytics of their performance. The smart-watch integration allows users to receive granular feedback on their workouts like vibrations for each rep and velocity and power measurements using the accelerometer. As the one member on our team with visual design and Illustrator experience, I ended up designing the screens of the final prototype.
